2. Understanding the Competition

S.W.C.D.UX.O. Profile

There is a plethora of gyms and personal trainers in the Colorado Springs area. I wanted to conduct a competitor analysis using the S.W.C.D.UX.O. outline to understand the strengths, weaknesses, content, design, user experience, and opportunities of direct and indirect competitors. For my direct competitors I looked up the top personal trainers in Colorado Springs on Yelp. This would give me a good idea of the top competition in the area and what they offer.
